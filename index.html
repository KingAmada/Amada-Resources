Who We Are

Incorporated in 2009, Amada Resources & Sons Ltd is an infrastructure development and general contracting company with proven competence in civil works, hydro engineering, power projects, and specialized procurement.

We seek to build long-lasting relationships with our customers by giving them the best deals in the real estate, construction, and supply industries. Our values are deeply rooted in integrity, excellent service, and maintaining a safe environment.

Our Mission

To create value for all stakeholders through qualitative service delivery centered in professional ethics and high standards from a culture of team work.
